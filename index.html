Profile

The Godfather is a classic crime drama that follows the powerful Italian-American Corleone mafia family. When the aging patriarch, Vito Corleone, is targeted by rival gangs, his reluctant son Michael is drawn into the violent underworld, eventually transforming into a ruthless leader to protect the family's legacy.
